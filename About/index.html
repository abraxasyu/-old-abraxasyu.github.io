About

Hi, my name is Sean Yu and I am a Clinical Informatics PhD Student in the Biomedical Sciences Graduate Program at the Ohio State University School of Medicine, in the department of Biomedical Informatics, under the guidance of Dr. Albert Lai and Dr. Courtney Hebert. My primary interest is developing clinical decision support applications using Electronic Health Record infrastructure and modern statistical techniques.

This is my personal blog mostly about my side projects.

The views and opinions you see here are mine and mine alone.
